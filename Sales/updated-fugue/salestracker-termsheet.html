General Terms & Conditions
1. The Earnest Money Deposit [EMD] must be paid in full to block the unit. There are multiple sales consultants & channel partners working on the sales of the project and if units are blocked for extended periods, we limit the options available for other customers. Hence the unit will be automatically released for sale to others in the market, if the signed Term Sheet AND full EMD is not received from you within 14 days from the offer date.
2. It is clearly agreed by the Intending Purchaser/s herein that this EMD amount is only for the purpose of blocking the home for a period of one month and the same shall be refunded after forfeiting an amount calculated at 4% of the Termsheet Value and the blocking automatically cancelled if the 1st installment of "Advance" is not paid within thirty days of blocking.
3. Definitive Agreements shall be signed up after the 1st installment of "Advance" is paid in full. Draft versions of the same are available on request.
4. These homes have been especially designed and detailed by "Shibanee & Kamal Architects". Any design decisions, whether interior or exterior, shall be at the sole discretion of "Shibanee& Kamal Architects". No external Architects/Designers shall be entertained. Before purchasing a home from us, please ensure that you are completely comfortable with our design philosophy.
5. The Carpet Area of a home shall be measured as the area where a carpet can be technically laid, before installation of woodwork and fenestration, and shall include the areas of bathrooms, balconies, utility spaces, internal stairs and gardens and 50% of the area of service platforms. At all locations, where a French window or door separates a room from a terrace garden, deck or balcony the carpet area shall include the door threshold area. In case of Terrace Gardens the internal area, excluding the peripheral beams, shall be included at 100%
6. In order to provide transparency and a standard measure to customers, Total Environment uses the Carpet Area of the apartment as a basis for calculating Saleable Area and has stayed away from the concept of Super Built-Up Area used by the Industry. However, to provide parity for price comparison, a standard divisor of 80% is applied on the Carpet Area to arrive at Saleable Area. Therefore, the Carpet Area is 80% of the Saleable Area.
7. The Built-Up Area shall include the entire apartment area measured to the center of any walls that are common with an adjacent apartment or the common area lobby, service area, exit stairs and elevator shafts, and to the outer face of all external walls. The Built-Up Area shall include bathrooms, balconies, utility spaces, internal stairs and terrace gardens and 50% of the area of service platforms, and all internal walls. Gardens, decks & water bodies, if built on concrete slabs shall be taken at 100%.
8. If you have selected standard customisation through eBuild, the customisation options shall be limited to those available through eBuild. If you have selected Personalised Customisation, options available through this shall be restricted at the sole discretion of our Architects. Further, such personal sessions with our Architects shall be limited to a maximum of 3 (Three) sessions running for a cumulative total of 9 (Nine) hours and these sessions are only possible at our office at 78, ITPL Road, Whitefield, Bangalore. The Personalised Customisation Option is available for a limited number of units only and, on first come first serve basis and therefore may not be available at a later point in time. Also, please note that In case, you have opted out from customisation, absolutely no single change shall be permitted from the standard plan and specifications, including any minor change requests.
9. The above unit can be transferred only after the 1st instalment of "Advance" has been paid in full AND the definitive Agreements signed. The unit can be transferred at a charge along with applicable taxes AND in such case, the potential Transferee would have to meet and be approved by the Developers. The applicable transfer charge shall be at 8% of Sale Consideration if transferred in the period up to the casting of the last slab of the tower in which the Unit is situated, 4% of Sale Consideration if transferred in the period between the casting of last slab of the tower in which the unit is situated till Virtual Completion of the Unit and 1% of the Sale Consideration if transferred in the period between Virtual Completion till registration of the Sale Deed in favour of the Purchasers.
10. The Handover date specified in this Term-Sheet is tentative and is dependent on the date of sign-off on your customisation.
11. The above consideration amount is inclusive of undivided Interest in the land as well as the construction of the home and the allocated car parks.
12. Land ownership shall be in the form of share of undivided interest in land only. Once your home is handed over, no further construction shall be possible at a later date.

  Structure
Shell RCC frame structure with masonry partitions. The height of each floor shall be 9'6'' or greater from slab to slab.
Masonry High quality exposed brick masonry using specially procured high-strength terracotta wire-cut bricks and /or stone masonry shall be provided for parts of the exteriors with deep-set waterproof pointing. For other parts and for the internal walls, terracotta blocks, table molded brick masonry or Aerocon blocks or Concrete block masonry with plaster shall be provided.
  Flooring & Dado
Living & Dining Beige coloured imported marble laid with paper joints and finished with 8 coats polish.
Kitchen Beige coloured imported marble laid with paper joints and finished with 8 coats polish. Granite counter with glazed tiles above the counter upto a height of 2'.
Family Space Wooden Laminate flooring.
Utility Green Marble flooring. No dado or counter shall be provided.
Bedrooms + Box Room Kota stone laid with paper joints and finished with 8 coats polish.
Other Bathrooms Brown Granite on the floor & counter and glazed tiles on the walls upto 7' in the shower area and 3' in other areas.
Wood Deck Brazilian Ipe or other natural hardwood, finished with oil.
Master Bathroom Imported marble on the floor and counter, Marazzi tiles on the walls upto 7' in shower area and 3' in other areas.
Maid's Room Kota stone laid with paper joints and finished with 8 coats polish.
Maid's Bathroom Green Marble on the floor and Glazed tiles on the walls to a height of 7'.
  Sanitary & Plumbing
Water Closets Western style, porcelain EWC of Kohler or equivalent make shall be provided in all bathrooms while a Parryware make EWC shall be provided in the maids bathroom.
Wash Basins Porcelain wash basins of Kohler or equivalent make shall be provided in all bathrooms. Parryware wash basin in the Maid's Bathroom. All basins shall be provided with a 32mm CP waste coupling & CP bottle trap of standard make.
Cockroach Traps A detachable stainless steel cockroach trap with lid of Chilly or equivalent make shall be provided in all bathrooms.
Bathtubs One Bathtub of Parryware / Woven Gold or equivaqlent make shall be provided in the Master bathroom.
Kitchen Sink Stainless steel sink with drainboard.
Faucets All faucets shall be CP, heavy body metal fittings of Grohe or equivalent make. Hot water connection shall be provided to the shower and wash basin in each bathroom and to the sink in the kitchen.
Overhead Showers Overhead showers of Jaquar or equivalent make shall be provided in all bathrooms.
Health Faucets Health faucets of Jaquar or equivalent make shall be provided in all bathrooms.
Plumbing All water supply lines & drainage lines shall be in PVC pipes. Sewage lines shall be in stoneware.
Washing Machine Point One cold water inlet and drainage outlet for a washing machine shall be provided in the Utility.
